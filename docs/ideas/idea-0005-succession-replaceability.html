Idea 0005: Succession & replaceability

  • Date: 2026-07-16
  • Status: seed
  • Parent: Idea 0001: Found the collaborative, decision-driven agentic way of working
  • Tags: succession, culture

The axis

Design the project so the driver can step back and the new generation owns it — knowledge and authority not bound to a person. The 3 young architects are the intended owners.

Why it matters

It is explicitly not about "me": when the work is done the driver wants to disappear cleanly and let the next generation take over. Key-person dependency would defeat both the collaboration goal and the beacon goal.

Open questions (seed)

  • What does "done" mean for a clean hand-over of ownership?
  • How does authority (accepting decisions, stewarding the method) transfer?
  • How do we surface and remove single-points-of-knowledge?
  • How do the young architects grow into owners along the way, not at the end?
