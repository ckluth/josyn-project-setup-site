Idea 0008: Relationship to the josyn field

  • Date: 2026-07-16
  • Status: seed
  • Parent: Idea 0001: Found the collaborative, decision-driven agentic way of working
  • Tags: migration, governance

The axis

How this collaborative reset relates to the existing josyn work — the sealed PoC josyn-platform (authoritative architecture, 45 ADRs) and the consolidation repo josyn-builder (decision-trail, target repo-world + classification vocabulary, with unfinished migration threads). What is inherited (adopt, don't re-litigate) versus re-opened for genuine team re-decision.

Why it matters

We do not start from scratch — much is already decided and much gold exists. The reset must neither waste that substance nor blindly inherit it past the team's scrutiny. This axis is the bridge from the human/method concerns to the technical field.

Open questions (seed)

  • What is frozen (inherited as-is) versus re-openable by the team?
  • How are inherited decisions ratified so the team genuinely owns them?
  • How does josyn-builder's unfinished migration continue — collaboratively, or handed off?
  • Note: source inventories scouted in docs/intermediate-artifacts/ feed this axis.
