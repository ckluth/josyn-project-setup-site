Idea 0007: Beacon vs. substance guardrail

  • Date: 2026-07-16
  • Status: seed
  • Parent: Idea 0001: Found the collaborative, decision-driven agentic way of working
  • Tags: agentic, guardrail

The axis

The project has two goals in tension: it is both the deliverable itself and a beacon / experimental field for agentic work. The guardrail: experimenting must not let the project substance suffer.

Why it matters

Without an explicit fence, the "experimental field" ambition can quietly erode the deliverable — and a damaged deliverable would discredit the whole agentic case with exactly the stakeholders we need to convince.

Open questions (seed)

  • How are experiments fenced from the deliverable substance?
  • What counts as "substance at risk", and who judges it?
  • How do we separate experiment from deliverable in practice (branches, repos, gates)?
  • How is a failed experiment rolled back without collateral damage?
