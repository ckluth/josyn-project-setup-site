Idea 0010: Roles, responsibilities & staffing

  • Date: 2026-07-17
  • Status: promoted
  • Parent: Idea 0001: Found the collaborative, decision-driven agentic way of working
  • Promoted to: ADR-0005: Roles, responsibilities & staffing
  • Tags: roles, responsibility, people, staffing

The axis

Who holds responsibility, what roles exist, the expectations and in-scope / out-scope boundaries for each — and the concrete staffing: which person fills which role right now. Responsibility cumulates in the driver as the main responsibility-holder — the one who reports to stakeholders, maintains project-progress, and keeps it all together. Explicitly not a team-lead by title — though in practice he will act like one: accountability without hierarchy. Alongside that single accountable role sit the other members' named roles, with each one's expectations and scope line. Staffing is a snapshot, changeable during the ride — roles are stable, the people in them are not.

Why it matters

Stakeholders need a single point of accountability, and a project without named roles diffuses responsibility until no one owns anything. Naming the roles is also the precondition for a clean succession hand-over and for legible progress. Recording the concrete staffing makes "who does what" visible today, while keeping it explicitly mutable so the trail never pretends the line-up is fixed.

Open questions (seed)

  • What is the main responsibility-holder accountable for — and, just as important, explicitly not accountable for?
  • How does "acts like a lead but is not a team-lead" stay genuinely non-hierarchical rather than a lead by another name?
  • What roles and expectations exist for the other members, and where is each one's in-scope / out-scope line drawn?
  • What is the right neutral name for the two younger architects, and what does their ownership track entail?
  • How does this relate to the repo-operator role (ADR-0004) — the same person, or a separable role?
  • How does the responsibility map feed the succession hand-over (Idea 0005), and who maintains progress per Idea 0009? It also shapes which audiences from Idea 0002 hold which role.
