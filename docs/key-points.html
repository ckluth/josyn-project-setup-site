Key points

A hand-curated TL;DR of the trail, grouped so a human reader gets the whole picture at a glance — one term, one sentence. A quick way in; the ideas and decisions themselves remain the source of truth.

Nothing here is settled yet. Every ADR is still proposed, and the Idea rows are earlier still — this is the current thinking, not ratified law. A row citing an ADR is a worked-through proposal; a row citing an Idea is a shaping thought not yet even proposed.

Vision & culture

Why this project exists, and the non-negotiable spirit it runs on.

Term In one sentence Source
The shift The move from an individual speed-boat to a collaborative project, re-deciding the essentials as a team rather than by decree. Idea 0001
Non-negotiable core Two things are not up for debate — the way of working is agentic and decision-driven. Idea 0001
Adoption by conviction The non-negotiables must be won by genuine buy-in across team, peers and authority — convince, never rule. Idea 0002
Beacon & guardrail The project is both a deliverable and an experimental beacon for agentic work, yet experimenting must never let the deliverable substance suffer. Idea 0007

People

Who is involved, who is accountable, and how the roles outlive their holders.

Term In one sentence Source
The driver One accountable, hand-over-able role reports to stakeholders, maintains project-progress, and keeps the whole coherent. ADR-0005
Accountability without hierarchy The driver holds no line authority and gets no command shortcut around the escalation ladder — caretaking, never command. ADR-0005
Roles vs. staffing Role definitions change through the ADR flow, but who fills a role is a mutable snapshot changed by a lightweight edit. ADR-0005
Succession by design The project is built so the driver can step back and the new generation owns it, with knowledge and authority never bound to a person. Idea 0005

Governance

How decisions get made — and kept honest.

Term In one sentence Source
By reason, not by boss Decisions are made by the best argument in the open, with authority as a fallback rather than the mechanism. ADR-0003
Team consensus Acceptance is a team act carried by an ADR's Decision Drivers and Considered Options, not one person's prerogative. ADR-0003
Escalation ladder When consensus genuinely fails, the decision passes to the Technical Lead, then — rarely ever — the Head of Dev. ADR-0003
Honest trail Decisions are recorded as they are made, never retrofitted, and questions surfacing in execution return to the decision stage. ADR-0003

Way of working

The method, and the day-to-day mechanics of collaborating.

Term In one sentence Source
Decision-trail We work idea → proposal → decision → plan → execution in plain markdown under docs/, so reasoning survives the session. ADR-0001
Talking is the collaboration The team collaborates by talking; the trail records the outcome and is not itself the review mechanism. ADR-0004
Repo-operator (curator) A single hand-over-able role holds the pen and commits the trail, serialising all writes to the repo. ADR-0004
Main-only The curator commits directly to main with no branches, PRs, or GitHub issues, keeping mechanics cheap. ADR-0004
No silent acceptance The proposed → accepted flip is the one guarded act — a dedicated commit, heading rename, and named acceptance record together. ADR-0004
True agile The way of working is genuinely adaptive, incremental, and feedback-driven — not agile ritual or cargo-cult ceremony. ADR-0004

Platform & tooling

Where the work lives, and how it stays reversible.

Term In one sentence Source
Repos are the source of truth Everything that matters — docs and code — lives as plain files in the git repositories, never locked inside a platform surface. ADR-0002
Reversibility A guaranteed, low-effort switch-back to Azure DevOps is a hard constraint that gates every tooling choice. ADR-0002
Portable An artifact is portable when its content and history survive a move to Azure DevOps by mechanical re-mapping, without loss of meaning. ADR-0002
GitHub as sandbox GitHub is a deliberately low-policy, experimental field; its proprietary surfaces (Issues, Projects) are disposable views, never authoritative. ADR-0002

The josyn field

The existing substance this plays out on, and how we relate to it.

Term In one sentence Source
Not from scratch The project builds on real existing substance — the sealed PoC josyn-platform and the consolidation repo josyn-builder. Idea 0008
Inherit vs. re-open What is inherited as-is versus genuinely re-opened for team re-decision is a deliberate choice, never a blind inheritance. Idea 0008

Project management

Milestones, progress-keeping and stakeholder reporting — still forming; only ADR crumbs so far, the rest lives in the ideas.

Term In one sentence Source
Phases & milestones Progress is carried in explicit, transparent phases and milestones — visible, incremental, reviewable — though the concrete set is not yet decided. Idea 0009
Stakeholder reporting The driver is the single address that reports project status outward to stakeholders. ADR-0005
Progress-keeping Maintaining project-progress is the driver's named accountability, so progress always has an owner. ADR-0005
Switch-back as a milestone The Azure DevOps switch-back is framed as a milestone criterion, its trigger deliberately not planned yet. ADR-0002
